# Wine 11 Podcast Plan

## The Show

**One sentence:** Wine 11 is three friends talking wine, life, luxury, beauty, motherhood, marriage, aging, and the things women say after the second glass.

**The vibe:** bougie, funny, honest, a little unfiltered, but not messy.

**Tagline:** Three friends. One bottle. Zero pretending.

Wine 11 is real talk on friendship, beauty, luxury, motherhood, midlife, and everything we only say after a pour.

---

## Episode Format (30–40 minutes)

| Segment | Time |
|---------|------|
| What we're drinking | 5 min |
| Main topic | 10 min |
| Funny story / confession | 10 min |
| Would you rather / Overrated or Underrated | 5 min |
| Takeaway or listener question | 5 min |

- **The Pour Decision**
- **Sip or Skip**

- **Things We're Too Old to Pretend to Like**
- **One Glass In**

## Pre-Launch Checklist

### Step 1: Record a 10-minute test episode (not for posting)
Use it to figure out:
- Who leads naturally
- Who interrupts
- Who brings the humor
- Who keeps structure
- Which topics feel strongest

### Step 2: Plan 6 episodes before recording
Ideas to start from:
- Things we thought we'd have figured out by now
- Is luxury self-care or a coping mechanism?
- Friendship in midlife hits different
- Beauty treatments we'd never admit we love
- Mom life after the kids don't need you the same way
- Wine, confidence, and the art of not caring so much

### Step 3: Record 3 episodes before launching
- **Episode 1:** Intro — who you are, why Wine 11
- **Episode 2:** Stronger topic
- **Episode 3:** Funniest or most relatable topic

## Production Setup

Film video, not just audio — growth will come from TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.

- Three chairs or a couch
- Wine glasses
- Cute lighting
- One iPhone on a tripod
- Clip-on mics if possible

---

## Content Strategy

### Short-form clips (10–15 per episode)
Post:
- Hot takes
- Funny one-liners
- Relatable midlife moments
- Mini debates
- Facial reactions

### Soft launch approach
Instead of "WE HAVE A PODCAST!!!" — post clips like:

> "This came up while recording Wine 11 and I can't stop thinking about it…"

### Weekly posting schedule
- 1 full episode
- 3–5 short clips
- 1 behind-the-scenes post
- 1 poll or question story

Commit to **8 weeks** before judging success.

---

## Growth Goals

1. First 100 engaged listeners
2. Then 500
3. Then 1,000 real fans

Don't chase viral immediately. Use the first couple months to improve chemistry, structure, editing, and branding.
